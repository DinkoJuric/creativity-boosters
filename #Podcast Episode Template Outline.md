# Podcast Episode Research Template Outline

## Title
Provide a concise and engaging title for the episode.

## Focus of the Episode
Briefly describe the central theme or topic that this episode will cover.

## Key Question to Explore
State the primary question that will guide the discussion throughout the episode.

## Business Relevance
Explain why this episode's topic matters for businesses, highlighting its significance and potential impact within a business context.

## Business Toolkit
### Exercise
Suggest a practical activity or exercise for listeners to apply the episode’s insights in their own business environment.

### Conversation Starter
Provide a question or prompt designed to initiate meaningful discussions within teams or organizations related to the episode’s theme.

### Metric Shift
Identify a key metric or indicator that listeners can track to measure the effect of implementing the episode’s takeaways.
