## ðŸ’¼ BUSINESS APPLICATION (6:00)

### The "Strategic Antenna" Concept
Every employee is exposed to signals executives miss:
- Customer complaints before they become trends
- Competitor moves observed in the field
- Operational bottlenecks that hide in dashboards

**Problem:** These insights rarely reach decision-makers
**Solution:** Create channels for strategic input at all levels

### How to Implement

| Level | Strategic Contribution |
|-------|----------------------|
| Frontline | "What are customers really saying?" |
| Middle Management | "What's blocking execution?" |
| Senior Leadership | "How do we synthesize these inputs?" |
| Executive | "How do we act on what we're hearing?" |

### Common Objections & Responses

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Employees don't understand strategy" | They understand customers and operations â€” that IS strategy |
| "Too many cooks" | Aggregate insights; one synthesizer decides |
| "No time" | One hour a month is 0.6% of work hours |
